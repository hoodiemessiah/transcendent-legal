02

What Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to run the app and save your progress. Nothing more.

  • Account Information — Your email address and display name, provided when you create an account. Used solely for authentication.
  • Spiritual Practice Data — Prayer completion records, dhikr counts, Qur'an reading progress, daily check-in data, and Muhasabah reflections. This data is stored to track your progression within the app.
  • Progression Data — Your XP, level, tier, streak count, Barakah, domain stats, and arc day. This is your game state.
  • Covenant & Intention — The personal intention you set during onboarding. Stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your account.
  • Device Identifiers — Standard identifiers provided by Firebase Authentication for account management. We do not collect your device's advertising ID.

We do not collect: your location, your contacts, your camera or microphone data, your browsing history, or any biometric information. We do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

✦

03

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your account across devices
  • To save and sync your spiritual practice progress
  • To calculate your tier, XP, Barakah, and progression milestones
  • To deliver the appropriate System Voice responses based on your state
  • To reset daily practice records at the appropriate time each day

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, behavioral analytics sold to third parties, or any purpose outside of operating Transcendent for your benefit.

✦

04

How Your Data is Stored

Your data is stored in two places:

  • On your device — Using AsyncStorage (React Native's local storage). This allows the app to function offline and persist your state between app launches.
  • In Firebase (Google Cloud) — When you create an account, your progress is synced to Firebase Firestore and authenticated via Firebase Authentication. Firebase is a Google Cloud product governed by Google's security and compliance standards. Data is stored in the United States.

Firebase's infrastructure is protected by industry-standard encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest. You can review Google Firebase's own privacy and security documentation at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

✦

05

Spiritual Data — Special Consideration

We recognize that your prayer records, Muhasabah reflections, and spiritual intentions are sensitive personal data. In many contexts this falls under religious belief data, which carries heightened privacy expectations.

We make a clear commitment: your spiritual practice data will never be sold, licensed, shared with advertisers, or used for any commercial purpose outside of operating Transcendent. This is a matter of Islamic ethics (amānah) as much as legal obligation.

Your Muhasabah text entries (if any are stored) are treated with the same confidentiality as private journal entries. They are not read by us, not used to train AI models, and not accessible to any third party.
